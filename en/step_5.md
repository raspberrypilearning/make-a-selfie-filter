## Add more

Make more accessories to follow your nose, mouth, ears, and more!

--- task ---

Duplicate your sprite:

- Right-click on your **accessory** sprite in the Sprite list
- Click on **duplicate**

(You could also add or paint a new sprite and copy the code.)

![The hat sprite in the Sprite list with the right-click menu showing, with 'duplicate' highlighted.](images/duplicate.png)

--- /task ---

--- task ---

Change the part of your face the new sprite follows:

- Click on the duplicated sprite
- In the code, change `top of head`{:class="block3extensions"} to a different option (such as `nose`{:class="block3extensions"}, `between eyes`{:class="block3extensions"}, `left ear`{:class="block3extensions"}, or `right ear`{:class="block3extensions"})

![In the script, the 'go to top of head' block has the drop-down menu open.](images/code4.png)

--- /task ---

Try these fun ideas:

- **Nose sticker** 👃: `go to [nose v]`{:class="block3extensions"}

- **Glasses 👓**: `go to [between eyes v]`{:class="block3extensions"}

- **Earrings** 👂: Make two sprites:
    - Left earring: `go to [left ear v]`{:class="block3extensions"}
    - Right earring: `go to [right ear v]`{:class="block3extensions"}
